Compliance Screener — Not Legal Advice. This tool is a preliminary compliance screening aid. It does not provide legal advice and does not conclusively determine copyright ownership. Under the Copyright Act (Canada) and the Berne Convention (150+ countries), copyright arises automatically upon creation of an original work — the absence of a notice does not mean an image is free to use. The real question for this business is whether it has verified authorization to use the image. Unclear or high-risk cases must be reviewed by legal counsel or a qualified manager before publication.
